Al Azhar Park

Cairo

Price: E£ 15

Built in may 2005 and listed as the top 60 greatest places in the world. Al azhar park is ana amazing place to spend some time with your family surrounded by nature.

Giza Zoo

Giza

Price: E£ 5

Opened by Khedive Ismail in 1891 covering an area of around 80 acers and home to over 170 species of animals. Giza zoo is the perfect place for animal lovers.

Cairo Tower

Giza

Price: E£ 25

The second most famous landmark in egypt after the pyramids, standing at 187 meters tall known to locals as Nassers Pineapple the tower oversees all of Cairo.

Cairo

The Egyptian Museum in Cairo is home to over 120,000 pieces of Anicient Egptian antiques. Designed by French architet Marcel Dourgnon and build in 1901.

Price: Free
