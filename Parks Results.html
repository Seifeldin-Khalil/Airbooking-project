Parks

With more than 20 million people living there, Cairo might seem like a noisy and crowded urban city. That may be true, but here are some of the city’s different parks and gardens that offer unique natural escapes where visitors can find beauty and peace in the midst of the city’s hustle and bustle.


Parks

Al Azhar Park

Salah Salem St, El-Darb El-Ahmar

Price: E£ 15

Built in may 2005 and listed as the top 60 greatest places in the world. Al azhar park is ana amazing place to spend some time with your family surrounded by nature.

Giza Zoo

Oula, Giza District

Price: E£ 5

Opened by Khedive Ismail in 1891 covering an area of around 80 acers and home to over 170 species of animals. Giza zoo is the perfect place for animal lovers.

Al Horreya Garden

Montazah Al Giza, Zamalek, Cairo

Price: E£ 25

Located across the street from the Cairo Opera House.The park is known for its 11 unique statues of hisotrical people who had impacted the history of their countries

Aquarium Grotto Garden

El Gabalaya St, Zamalek

Price: Starting from E£ 80

Also known as El Gabalaya Garden built in 1867 by Khedivi Ismail. Known for its unique types of fish from the nile

Gezira Island

Giza

Price: Starting from E£ 200

Located in the nile in central cairo.First known as Garden of Plants home to many to amazing natural scenery.

Al-Andalus Garden

Al Abageyah, El Mukkatam

Price: Varies based on person

Formally known as El Tahrir Garden. Known for its unique Andalusian styled architecture and statues of pharos and Ahmed Shawky.

Japanese Garden

Mohammed Moustafa Al Maraghi, Helwan

Price: Free

Built in 1917 to represent the cultures of the east.Full of fish ponds, various tree types and more than 30 types of Buddha statues.

Orman Garden

Giza

Price: Starting from E£ 30

One of the worlds most famous botanical gardens built in 1875.Was home to over 150 types of flowers and more than 500 types of plants.

Al Fustat Garden

Salah Salem St, Ein as Seirah

Price: Free

Located next to Al Azhar park. Al Fustat Garden is Egypt largest garden. The garden is a green heaven for nature lovers.
